Who We Are

Pearl 12-77 Multilateral Systems Limited (Pearl 12-77 Limited) a dynamic technology-based international freight forwarding and trading company incorporated as a LLC in Nigeria, to provide professional Import and Export services; Logistics services; International trading services and ICT Education and development.


A company established, based on the objective of bridging the gap existing in the area of connecting people who needs our services and products through the provision of a seamless and efficient service delivery.


With headquarter based in Lagos (Nigeria), we serve countries such as China, Germany, Greece, Turkey, UK and USA via our long standing and very reliable and capable partners.


Pearl 12-77 has the expertise and human resources to handle all freight forwarding requirements and provide shipping services that ensure that your goods arrive in perfect condition.

    The focal point of our business operations is based on our presence at the Apapa Sea Port; Muritala Mohammed International Airport, Lagos and Nnamdi Azikwe International Airport, Abuja - Nigeria.


    Our operations involve the shipment of Export consignments to different locations across the World and Import consignments handling in Nigeria.


    We personalize services to meet specific needs of individual customers in the following areas:-

    Customs Clearance (Fast track):-

    Pearl 12-77 offer professional Customs Clearance services for import or export consignments by Air and Ocean at all major Air and Seaports. Our experience staff works closely with clients to help them avoid costly penalties and delays while the goods are under customs hold by ensuring fast and smooth clearance of cargo.

    We offer a complete portfolio of brokerage services for both Import and Export custom clearance to our clients (including duty drawback, full product line classification, compliance management, incentives from dgft, etc.). Our hands-on interface with Customs and other government agencies through the established Broker Interface permits us to clear your shipment immediately on landing.

    This allows us to streamline the movement of your cargo and further reduce your overall cycle time and cost saving proposition.


    Project Cargo Consolidation:-

    Project freight forwarding remains Pearl 12-77 limited core business, focusing predominantly on industries related to Energy, Power Generation, Mining & Metals and Infrastructure projects. We can offer a seamless portfolio of support services from the initial planning, budgetary phase to the final delivery and facility start up.

    Our expert team designs to offer effective handling of project shipment in meeting client’s requirements. We undertake feasibility and engineering studies for heavy‐lift, over‐dimensional and/or critical items for the project. Perform route surveys to ensure that the best concepts for the project as they relate to cost, performance, safety and operational excellence.


    Diplomatic Cargo Consolidation:-

    Pearl 12-77 Limited offers its customers a complete logistics in handling of all ODD Dimensional (ODC) cargo and offers all necessary allied activities to meet customers’ requirements to include:

  • Freight booking for special cargo, ODD Dimensional (ODC) stuffed in open tops and flat racks / flat beds

  • Customs broking for special cargoes, heavy lifts and break-bulk cargo.

  • Transportation of heavy lifts and Odd Dimensional (ODC) cargoes from customer warehouse to port and vice versa.

  • Stuffing, Lashing and choking of the cargo as per requirements of the port, liner and customer.

  • Door to Door deliveries from shippers warehouse to consignees warehouse at destination through our network of offices.

  • We cater for the need of our customers in the area of providing same day air freight delivery services for urgent parcel/cargo consignment.

  • Also we cater for customers need and request in the areas of Reservation and Ticketing; Airport Shuttle Services; Air craft Charter Services.


    • Commercial Cargo Consolidation:-

    Pearl 12-77 Limited offers worldwide less-than-container load service to meet customers’ requirement when they don’t have enough cargo for a Full Container Load (FCL). We packed your cargo separately and group several such packages together in accordance to their destination after ensuring compatibility with other cargo. The cargo is then transshipped to its final destination.

    Pearl 12-77 Limited is able to deliver and receive cargo to and from across the world. Our focus is on offering direct services across the world in the following areas.

  • Outbound Consolidation: Our export Consolidation Programmed is designed to cater to movement of LCL Shipments direct China, Europe, & Other Destinations. We have LCL services major ports. Our expert operational staff carefully handles the LCL cargoes at our warehouses to avoid damages at the time of offloading and stacking. They further ensure that the same are properly stuffed into the containers and are adequately secured to ensure safe handling.

  • Inbound Consolidation: Our Import Cargo Consolidation Programmed is designed to meet growing needs of Importers in Nigeria to choose the shortest route to move their cargoes in the shortest possible transit time keeping in mind the constraint of Cost & Safety.

  • Buyer Consolidation: Pearl 12-77 Limited offers consolidation service for buyers to consolidate shipments from various shippers into one full-container load shipment. Cargo consolidations not only free our customers of certain complicated tasks but it also saves them a considerable amount of time and expenses.
